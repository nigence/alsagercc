Summary of how it went:
- 4 short and quick chess games over an 8-day period arranged "Swiss style" between 11 and 18 April.
- Covid-19 themed prizes and (optional) publicity for the top scoring players in each category.
- Categories are simple: (1)chess club members; (2)non-members; and (3)junior non-members.
- Entry is free.
- It's time-flexible : arrange to play each game in a 2-day window to suit you.
- Ideal for young players to get a taste of tournament chess.

Depending on individual performances, each game could last between 1 and 40 minutes (15|10 time control).

To enter, simply create a (free) account on chess.com and send your username to secretary@alsagerchessclub.org.uk or message via www.chess.com at "ClubPaperJack" before 5pm on Friday 10 April.

Support via telephone is available if you need help getting started on the website etc.

At the start of each round, the username "handle" of your opponent is messaged to you; challenge them on the website or accept their challenge if they got there first.
