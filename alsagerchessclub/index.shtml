We are a friendly and established chess club about half way between Crewe and Stoke on Trent.

Although small, we have a couple of teams in the NSDCA divisions 3 and 5.

We'd love to get more people involved - either young or old, novices or seasoned players, or any level between.

If you'd like to play chess for fun, improve your game, or take part in competitive league chess, come along or contact us.
