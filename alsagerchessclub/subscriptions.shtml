subscriptions

Subscriptions are due from 1st September. Standard adult membership is £30.00 (pro rata reductions, each month, for new members arriving later). Juniors' membership (u18) is free. Patrons' membership is £50.00 (or as much as you can manage!)

We are most thankful to our donors and Alsager Cricket Club for their invaluable support.

Our venue (Alsager Cricket Club) is a private members club; but, our bona fide members, and league opponents, are granted associate membership.

For league gamers, please also acquire English Chess Federation membership at least at "Bronze" level. Currently, this is about 18 pounds per year for seniors, free for first year juniors, and 6 pounds for juniors in following years.
